General Root [Smali Patcher]

WARNING: Flashing a custom recovery to your device will void the warranty.
We take no responsibility for any damage that may occur.

Flashing TWRP is device specific. This guide will just help you to understand the process better.
You still need to find the proper USB Drivers and Flash Tool.

Download files

  1. Save the appropriate 'TWRP' to you computer
    Download it here: https://twrp.me/Devices/
    Device not listed? Search on xda-developers for a TWRP
  2. A program to flash TWRP to your device.
    Brand specific » Search Google
  3. USB drivers of your device.
    Brand specific » Search Google
  4. Extract Smali Patcher to you computer
    Download from xda-developers
  5. Save magisk to your computer.
    Download from xda-developers

Flash TWRP and install Magisk

  1. Enable developer options
    Go to Settings » About Device » Software Info and tap 7x on 'Build Number'
  2. Now go to Settings » Developer options.
    You need to enable [Enable OEM unlock] and [USB Debugging].
    (Can't find OEM Unlock? No problem, proceed)
  3. Flash TWRP to your device. This is brand specific, search google on how to do it exactly.
    After you flashed TWRP and you booted TWRP the first time, don't close TWRP, proceed to the next step.
  4. You will reach a screen asking you if you want to allow system modifications.
    Swipe to the right.
  5. ** DO NOT EXIT TWRP AFTER THIS STEP, GO TO MAIN MENU AGAIN **
    Go to [Wipe] » [Format Data] and type 'yes' as instructed and go back to the main menu.
  6. Without exiting TWRP, transfer the Magisk zipfile to your device
  7. Flash the Magisk-zip using [Install] in TWRP.
  8. Reboot to system once the zip is installed.

    9. Wait 2-5 minutes for your device to finish setting itself up.

  9. Check if your device contains the app Magisk Manager.
    If not, download the [Magisk Manager] apk.
    Download from xda-developers
  10. Hide the Magisk manager.
    • Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
    • Go to Settings
    • Tap Hide Magisk Manager
    • Magisk Manager should close in order to change the package name (to Manager).
    • You can check if it was sucessful by repeating the same steps and checking if now instead of Hide Magisk Manager it says Restore Magisk Manager.
  11. Hide Magisk for Pokemon GO
    • Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
    • Go to Magisk Hide
    • There you will find a list of your installed apps.
    • Check the square next to Pokemon GO.
    • That's it. Pokemon GO shouldn't detect root (Magisk) anymore.

Smali Patcher to mock location

  1. Connect your device to your computer.
  2. Authorize USB debugging connection on your device
  3. Run "SmaliPatcher.exe", First startup will automatically download the latest necessary binary's
  4. Select your desired patches, but certainly check [Mock locations]
  5. Hit "ADB PATCH" button
  6. Once the process has completed, magisk module will be generated in the same directory as "SmaliPatcher.exe" named: "SmaliPatcherModule-X.X.X.X-fOmey@XDA.zip".
  7. Install the generated zip file as a module in Magisk Manager.
  8. Reboot after that.

Install GPS Joystick

  1. Download GPS Joystick from the App Ninjas
    • Go to the App Ninjas website.
    • Select the APK for your device's architecture.
      To figure out your architecture download CPU-Z from the playstore.
    • Install it
  2. Open the GPS Joystick app
  3. Tap on [Privacy Mode] » Generate and give it a name.
  4. Confirm that your new app is created an delete the old one (GPS Joystick).
  5. Now open your device's Settings » Developer options.
    You need to set your app in [Set mock location app].
  6. Go back to Settings.
  7. Tap Connections » Location
    • Locating method » [Device Only]
    • Improve accuracy » Disable all
    • Google Location History » Disable (pause)
  8. Disable [Find My Phone] (you can search for 'Find' in settings.)
  9. Now open your previously renamed GPS Joystick app.
    • Tap on the menu.
    • Go to Settings.
    • Disable [Enable Indirect Mocking]
  10. That's it. You're finished and ready to spoof.
