General

What's the minimum Android version I need?

At the time of writing, it's still possible to spoof on Andriod with at least version 4.0.
But Niantic will stop supporting Android version 4.x soon.

Which security patch level do I have?

  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Near the bottom, tap System » Advanced » System update.
    If you don't see Advanced tap About phone » Software info.
  3. See your "Security patch level".

Google Play Services

Which Google Play Services version do I you need?

 Click to enlarge
  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Tap Apps » All Apps » Google Play Services.
  3. In this example we can see my current Google Play Services Version:16.0.89.
    Followed by (040408-...) That number (040408) is the one you need.
    Obviously your phone has a different one.
    Sometimes certain phones only show 448 for example so you put a 0 before each number making it 040408.

How to stop auto-update Google Play Services?

  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Tap Apps » All Apps » Google Play Services.
  3. Tap on Mobile data » Disable [Allow background data usage].
  4. Go back to the previous screen.
  5. Scroll down for [Permissions] » Disable [Storage].
  6. Go back to the previous screen.
  7. Tap on Storage » Clear Cache
  8. Now open Settings » Apps » All Apps » Google Play Store
  9. Tap [Disable] to disable the Google Play Store
  10. Go back to Settings » Apps
  11. In the right corner click the 3 dots.
  12. » [Show system apps]
  13. Now search for Download Manager and tap on it
  14. Tap [Disable] to disable Download Manager
  15. Restart your phone

Magisk

How to hide Magisk Manager?

  1. Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Hide Magisk Manager
  4. Magisk Manager should close in order to change the package name (to Manager).
  5. You can check if it was sucessful by repeating the same steps and checking if now instead of Hide Magisk Manager it says Restore Magisk Manager.

How to hide Magisk for Pokemon GO?

  1. Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
  2. Go to Magisk Hide
  3. There you will find a list of your installed apps.
  4. Check the square next to Pokemon GO.
  5. That's it. Pokemon GO shouldn't detect root (Magisk) anymore.
