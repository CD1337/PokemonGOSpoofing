General

Which security patch level do I have?

  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Near the bottom, tap System and then Advanced and then System update.
    If you don't see Advanced tap About phone and then Software info.
  3. See your "Security patch level".

Google Play Services

Which Google Play Services version do I you need?

 Click to enlarge
  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Tap Apps and then All Apps and then Google Play Services.
  3. In this example we can see my current Google Play Services Version:16.0.89.
    Followed by (040408-...) That number (040408) is the one you need.
    Obviously your phone has a different one.
    Sometimes certain phones only show 448 for example so you put a 0 before each number making it 040408.

Magisk

How to hide Magisk Manager?

  1. Open Magisk Manager and click on the menu.
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Hide Magisk Manager
  4. Magisk Manager should close in order to change the package name (to Manager).
  5. You can check if it was sucessful by repeating the same steps and checking if now instead of Hide Magisk Manager it says Restore Magisk Manager.

How to hide Magisk for Pokemon GO?

  1. Open Magisk Manager and click on the menu.
  2. Go to Magisk Hide
  3. There you will find a list of your installed apps.
  4. Check the square next to Pokemon GO.
  5. That's it. Pokemon GO shouldn't detect root (Magisk) anymore.
