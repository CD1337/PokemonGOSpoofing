Download files

  1. Save the appropriate 'TWRP' to you computer
    Download it here: https://twrp.me/Devices/
    Device not listed? Search on xda-developers for a TWRP
  2. Download and extract "Mi Unlock Tool" your computer
    Download from MIUI website
  3. Install the 'Minimal ADB and Fastboot' to your computer.
    Download from AndroidFileHost
  4. Extract Smali Patcher to you computer
    Download from xda-developers
  5. Save magisk to your computer.
    Download from xda-developers

Flash TWRP and install Magisk

  1. Enable developer options
    Go to Settings » About Device » tap 7x on 'MIUI Version'
  2. Now go back to settings and scroll down to "Accounts" » Tap "MI Account" and Login to your MI account if you haven't
    Make sure "Sync" and "Find My Device" is enabled.
  3. Now go to Settings » Additional Settings » Developer options.
    You need to enable [Enable OEM unlock] and [USB Debugging].
  4. Open Mi Unlock and Sign in to your Mi Account (the same one as your mobile phone).
  5. Reboot your device into Download mode.
    To do this, power off your device and when he's off, hold the [Volume Down] + [Power] buttons.
  6. Connect your phone to your computer.
  7. Miui Unlock Tools program should detect your phone and all you have to do is click "Unlock". A warning should appear saying that all the data will be erased just click "Unlock anyway".
  8. Once the Unlock is successful click the "Restart phone" button.
    Some People might get an error at 99% where it says you must wait for 72 Hours(or some other random time). Unfortunately there's no workaround that and you will have to wait the required time.
  9. The phone will take some time to restart. Once it reboots you'll have to retype your Miui password and click "Activate" and do the initial basic phone setup.
    If you wanna see a video to understand the proccess better do so here : https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Sru7VjZzCCg
  10. Now to proceed to flash TWRP you'll have to repeat the steps: 1-3
  11. Reboot your device into Download mode again. (Just like in step 5) and connect your phone to the computer.
  12. Open "Minimal ADB and Fastboot" shortcut on your Desktop
  13. Write the command: fastboot devices
    Followed by: fastboot flash recovery twrp.img
    Note: You'll have to change the twrp file you downloaded to twrp.img and move it to the "Minimal ADB and Fastboot" instalation folder or adjust the command accordingly. For example if you have the twrp file downloaded in your downloads folder like c:\users\username\downloads you can adjust the command to : fastboot flash recovery c:\users\username\downloadstwrp.img
  14. After TWRP is installed hold [Vol up] + [Power] buttons
  15. You will reach a screen asking you if you want to allow system modifications.
    Swipe to the right.
  16. ** DO NOT EXIT TWRP AFTER THIS STEP, GO TO MAIN MENU AGAIN **
    Go to [Wipe] » [Format Data] and type 'yes' as instructed and go back to the main menu.
  17. Without exiting TWRP, transfer the Magisk zipfile to your device
  18. Flash the Magisk-zip using [Install] in TWRP.
  19. Reboot to system once the zip is installed.

    20. Wait 2-5 minutes for your device to finish setting itself up.

  20. Check if your device contains the app Magisk Manager.
    If not, download the [Magisk Manager] apk.
    Download from xda-developers
  21. Hide the Magisk manager.
    • Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
    • Go to Settings
    • Tap Hide Magisk Manager
    • Magisk Manager should close in order to change the package name (to Manager).
    • You can check if it was sucessful by repeating the same steps and checking if now instead of Hide Magisk Manager it says Restore Magisk Manager.
  22. Hide Magisk for Pokemon GO
    • Open Magisk Manager and tap on the menu.
    • Go to Magisk Hide
    • There you will find a list of your installed apps.
    • Check the square next to Pokemon GO.
    • That's it. Pokemon GO shouldn't detect root (Magisk) anymore.

Smali Patcher to mock location

  1. Connect your device to your computer.
  2. Authorize USB debugging connection on your device
  3. Run "SmaliPatcher.exe", First startup will automatically download the latest necessary binary's
  4. Select your desired patches, but certainly check [Mock locations]
  5. Hit "ADB PATCH" button
  6. Once the process has completed, magisk module will be generated in the same directory as "SmaliPatcher.exe" named: "SmaliPatcherModule-X.X.X.X-fOmey@XDA.zip".
  7. Install the generated zip file as a module in Magisk Manager.
  8. Reboot after that.

Install GPS Joystick

  1. Download GPS Joystick from the App Ninjas
    • Go to the App Ninjas website.
    • Select the APK for your device's architecture.
      To figure out your architecture download CPU-Z from the playstore.
    • Install it
  2. Open the GPS Joystick app
  3. Tap on [Privacy Mode] » Generate and give it a name.
  4. Confirm that your new app is created an delete the old one (GPS Joystick).
  5. Now open your device's Settings » Developer options.
    You need to set your app in [Set mock location app].
  6. Go back to Settings.
  7. Tap Connections » Location
    • Locating method » [Device Only]
    • Improve accuracy » Disable all
    • Google Location History » Disable (pause)
  8. Disable [Find My Phone] (you can search for 'Find' in settings.)
  9. Now open your previously renamed GPS Joystick app.
    • Tap on the menu.
    • Go to Settings.
    • Disable [Enable Indirect Mocking]
  10. That's it. You're finished and ready to spoof.
