Downgrade Google Play Services

The version that will work on your phone is unknown but there are a few versions that you can try.
Those are 12.5.29, 12.6.85, 12.6.87 and 12.6.88. Stick with the one that works for you.

Install FGL Pro

  1. Install FGL Pro from the Play Store
  2. Now open your device's Settings » Developer options.
    You need to set FGL Pro in [Set mock location app].
  3. Go back to Settings.
  4. Tap Connections » Location
    • Locating method » [Device Only]
    • Improve accuracy » Disable all
    • Google Location History » Disable (pause)

Stop auto-update and downgrade

  1. Open your device's Settings.
  2. Disable [Find My Phone] (you can search for 'Device Administrator' in settings.)
  3. Go back to Settings » Apps » All Apps » Google Play Services.
  4. Tap on Mobile data » Disable [Allow background data usage].
  5. Go back to the previous screen.
  6. Tap [Disable] to uninstall your updated Google Play Services.
  7. Now install your previous downloaded Google Play Services
  8. Then go back to Settings » Apps » All Apps » Google Play Services.
  9. Scroll down for [Permissions] » Disable [Storage].
  10. Go back to the previous screen.
  11. Tap on Storage » Clear Cache
  12. Now open Settings » Apps » All Apps » Google Play Store
  13. Tap [Disable] to disable the Google Play Store
  14. Go back to Settings » Apps
  15. In the right corner click the 3 dots.
  16. » [Show system apps]
  17. Now search for Download Manager and tap on it
  18. Tap [Disable] to disable Download Manager
  19. Reboot your phone
  20. That's it. You're finished and ready to spoof.
